Who am I?

Barbara Cusumano is a 34-year-old Argentinean who has been traveling around the world for the past five years. She has lived in Amsterdam for a while now, but before that, she spent a year in Ireland and three years in Australia. Her passion for exploring new places and meeting new people has taken her to some of the most beautiful cities and hidden gems around the world.

Apart from her love for traveling, Barbara is an avid fan of Harry Potter and murder documentaries. She can spend hours discussing the intricacies of the magical world of Hogwarts and debating the motives of serial killers. Barbara has always been fascinated by cult documentaries, and she is a devoted fan of the genre. For her, these films offer a unique window into some of the most intriguing and mysterious subcultures of our time. Whether it's exploring the world of UFO enthusiasts, conspiracy theorists, or religious fanatics, cult documentaries have a way of shining a light on the fringes of society and revealing the strange and sometimes disturbing beliefs that lurk there. In her free time, Barbara loves to watch tennis grand slams, especially when her favorite player is on the court.

Barbara's love for travel and adventure has taken her to many new places, but she has never lost sight of the importance of family and friends. She values the relationships she has with her loved ones above all else and makes sure to keep in touch with them no matter where in the world she is. Whenever Barbara visits a new city or country, she takes the time to learn about the local culture and connect with the people who live there. She has made many friends along the way, some of whom have become like family to her. These relationships have enriched her life and given her a sense of belonging in the places she visits.

Despite being far away from her loved ones, Barbara makes an effort to keep in touch with them regularly. She uses social media and video calls to stay connected and share her experiences with them. Whenever she returns home, she spends time catching up with her family and friends, and they are always eager to hear about her latest adventures.
