Amsterdam

Barbara Cusumano has been living in Amsterdam since June 2022, and she is absolutely loving the city. Amsterdam is a vibrant and diverse city with a rich history, beautiful architecture, and a thriving cultural scene. The city has a lot to offer, including world-class museums, delicious food, and a vibrant nightlife.

One of the things that makes Amsterdam a great option as a place to live is its excellent quality of life. The city is known for its bike-friendly streets, clean air, and excellent healthcare system. It is also a very safe city, with low crime rates and a welcoming and inclusive community.

Barbara is particularly drawn to Amsterdam's art scene, and she spends much of her free time exploring the city's many galleries and museums. The Van Gogh Museum and the Rijksmuseum are two of her favorites, and she has also discovered many lesser-known galleries and exhibition spaces that showcase contemporary art and emerging artists.
